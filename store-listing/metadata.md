## Description (4000 chars max)

TripCopilot is your intelligent travel companion that keeps you ahead of every flight change, delay, and travel detail.

**Real-Time Flight Tracking**
Add your flights and get instant status updates. TripCopilot monitors your flights and sends push notifications for delays, cancellations, and gate changes — so you're never caught off guard.

**Smart Notifications**
- Morning briefings on travel days with weather and flight status
- Check-in reminders 24 hours before departure
- Pre-flight alerts 3 hours before takeoff
- Hotel check-in reminders
- Packing suggestions based on destination weather
- Passport expiry warnings

**Trip Management**
Organize all your trips in one place. Track flights, hotels, and ground transportation. Share trip cards with friends and family.

**Travel Tools**
- Visa Checker: Instant visa requirements for 140+ country combinations
- Expense Tracker: Log travel expenses in 9 currencies with receipt photos
- Boarding Pass Wallet: Store boarding passes offline for airport access
- Passport Expiry Tracker: Never miss a renewal deadline
- AI Trip Journal: Auto-generated travel summaries

**Social Features**
Connect with fellow travelers, share your travel map, and see who's been where you're going.

**Two Modes**
- Relax Mode: Clean, simple interface with smart alerts
- Pilot Mode: Full aviation dashboard with live delay feeds and weather data

**Privacy First**
Expenses, boarding passes, and passport data stay on your device. No ads. No data selling.
